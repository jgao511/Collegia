Birthday paradox

A match arrives sooner than intuition expects.

As each new person enters, they compare birthdays with everyone already in the room. The number of pairs grows quickly, so matches become surprisingly likely.

Monte Carlo estimation

Estimate pi by throwing random points at a circle.

Random points land inside a quarter circle about pi/4 of the time. Multiply that hit rate by 4, and the estimate settles toward pi.

pi ≈ 4 × inside ÷ total
